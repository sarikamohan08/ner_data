### AGE GROUP EXTRACTION
===============================================================

Extract the Age Group exactly as written in the text.

Age Group must ALWAYS be a TEXT FIELD.  
Do NOT convert to numbers.  
Do NOT infer any missing values.

Extraction Rules:
1. Capture ANY phrase that indicates age, age range, age group, or age category.

3. If multiple age groups appear, return all separated by commas.
4. **If NO age-related text is found anywhere in the document, leave `age_group` as an empty string ("").**
